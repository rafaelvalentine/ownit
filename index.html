Cooperative mission

our vision and philosophy

The BOSSCOOP provides individuals with an instrument of self-help and influence over local communities, regions, wealth creation and redistribution, democracy, empowerment, and by producing an ever-increasing convergence between the economic, social and environmental components of development.

Services

Business and Technology Strategies

provides valuable research, collaboration and resources to help Cooperative members successfully operate, optimize and transform their systems and relationships with member-owners in our evolving industry.

Communications

Communications helps cooperative members plan and execute strategic communications programs and builds support for co-op issues through digital channels and media outreach.

Employee Benefits

From employer-sponsored group insurance to retirement plans, BOSSCOOP offers a one-source solution for the best value in comprehensive benefit programs for co-op members

Government Relations

Government Relations advocates on behalf of BOSSCOOP members on federal legislative and regulatory matters and in national policy forums, and develops and trains our grassroots to impact policies at the federal, state and local level.

Market Research

The BOSSCOOP Market Research Services team is led by professionals who are well-schooled in market research techniques, understand the business of your cooperatives and care about your success.
